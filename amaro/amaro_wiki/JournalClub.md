# Amaro Lab Journal Club Series[edit](</mediawiki/index.php?title=JournalClub&action=edit&section=1> "Edit section: Amaro Lab Journal Club Series")]

The Amaro lab holds a journal club session once every month. 

The time for a journal club should be approximately one hour. Attendance is strongly encouraged, and is intended to help all lab members gain exposure and have discussions around journal articles relevant to our research. 

Default venue will be on Zoom, unless specified otherwise. Recording of the session is preferable, but not required, depending on the comfort level of the facilitator and attendees.
