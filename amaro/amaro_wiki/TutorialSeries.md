# Amaro Lab Tutorial Series[edit](</mediawiki/index.php?title=TutorialSeries&action=edit&section=1> "Edit section: Amaro Lab Tutorial Series")]

Starting in the Winter of 2023, the Amaro lab holds a tutorial session once every two weeks (~5 per quarter). 

The time for each tutorial can range from 30 to 90 minutes. Attendance is optional, and is primarily intended to help lab members learn new skills. Volunteers from outside of the lab are welcome to attend and even facilitate the tutorials, be they collaborators, friends, or industry contacts. 

The tutorials can be a workshop, theory class, hands-on type tutorial, or just a simple journal club discussion about broad or specific topics. 

Default venue will be on Zoom, unless specified otherwise. Recording of the session is preferable, but not required, depending on the comfort level of the facilitator and attendees.
