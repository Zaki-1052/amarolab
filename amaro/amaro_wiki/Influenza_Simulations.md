#### Goals[edit](</mediawiki/index.php?title=Influenza_Simulations&action=edit&section=3> "Edit section: Goals")]
    
    
     - Calculate the propensity of HA and NA raft formation with respect to various influenza strains
       - What are the effects of rafts on overall membrane electrostatics
     - Study the effects of membrane biophysics
       - Is there lipid reordering due to membrane protein
    

#### Simulations[edit](</mediawiki/index.php?title=Influenza_Simulations&action=edit&section=4> "Edit section: Simulations")]
    
    
     - Naked influenza membrane without any protein
       - Do we see lipid movement contrary to what Tyler saw
     - HA + Membrane 
       - Is there any lipid reordering
       - How do the CG vs AA ensemble electrostatics compare?
