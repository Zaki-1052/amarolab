# Docking Programs

### Docking Programs[edit](</mediawiki/index.php?title=Docking_Programs&action=edit&section=1> "Edit section: Docking Programs")]

Molecular Dynamics: CHARMM, NAMD, GROMACS, Tinker, NWChem?, etc. 

Docking: AutoDock, Dock, DOT, etc. 

There are two distinct types of docking here: Ligand-Protein docking and Protein-Protein docking. The Ligand-Protein docking can be further split into those that are more appropraite for virtual screening and those that are more appropriate for (so-called) rigorous docking.
